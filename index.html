Problem Statement

Many modern buildings waste large amounts of energy due to inefficient heating, ventilation, and air conditioning (HVAC) systems. This project aims to design and test a small-scale airflow control system that reduces power consumption while maintaining comfortable temperatures. By improving airflow dynamics and system efficiency, this project seeks to lower energy costs and reduce environmental impact.
