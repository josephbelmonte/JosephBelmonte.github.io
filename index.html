Highlighted Projects

Project Management 1: HTML & CSS Setup

This stage focused on creating the website foundation using HTML and CSS. The site introduces my project addressing HVAC airflow inefficiency in modern buildings — a problem that leads to wasted energy and high operational costs.

Project Management 2: Problem Statement

Inefficient HVAC systems waste significant amounts of energy and money due to poor airflow control. The goal is to design a smart, small-scale airflow control system that adjusts automatically to maintain comfort while lowering power consumption.

Project Management 3: Affinity Diagram

Our team brainstormed over 20 ideas and grouped them into clusters covering technology, cost, sensors, automation, maintenance, and user comfort. The Affinity Diagram helped us visualize possible approaches and challenges for implementing an efficient HVAC airflow control system.

Project Management 4: Solution Sketches

This phase includes conceptual sketches of the smart HVAC control system, illustrating user interface ideas, airflow sensors, and the adaptive control dashboard. These visuals show how the solution would operate in real-world building environments.
